Facts and Questions

If you have any problems concerning anything about our website whether functionality-wise or topic-wise, consult the FAQs here.

What is Literary Oasis?

Literary Oasis is an online platform where users can explore, borrow, and manage a collection of books easily from one place.

Do I need an account to borrow books?

Yes, you must create an account and log in to borrow books and access personalized features like your borrowed list.

How do I borrow a book?

Simply browse the available books, click on your preferred title, and select the “Borrow” button. The book will then appear in your borrowed section.

Can I return a borrowed book?

Yes, you can return a book anytime from your borrowed books page by clicking the “Return” option.
